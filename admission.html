Admission Instruction

Welcome to Suhani English Boarding School-where we nurture young minds through academic excellence, discipline, and holistic development. Admissions are now open for the upcoming academic session.

Step 1: Initial Inquiry

Visit our school or contact our admission office. Our team is available for Sunday to Friday from 6:00 AM to 4:10 PM to assist you.

Step 2: Application Form

Collect the Collect the application form from the school office or fill it online website: Admission Form Submit the filled form with the required documents.

Step 3: Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • Two recent passport-size
  • Latest academic report card and CC
  • ID proof of parents/guardians
  • Medical or immunization record

Step 4: Entrance Test / Interview

  • Play Group to Grade I:Informal interaction with the child and parents
  • Grade II and above:Written test in English, Math, Science, and General Knowledge, followed by an interview

Step 5: Admission Decision

Selected candidates will be notified by phone, email, or notice board. Admission is based on merit and seat availability.

Step 6: Confirmation & Fee Payment

Parents must pay the admission fee within the given timeframe to confirm enrollment. Document verification is required.

Step 7: Orientation and Joining

Orientation details, book lists, and uniform guidelines will be shared before the session starts.

Important Notice

  • Minimum age and eligibility criteria apply as per school policy.
  • Admissions depend on seat availability and performance.
  • Be sure you are filling proper and true informations.

About Us

Suhani English Boarding School is one of the pioneering institutions committed to providing affordable and high-quality education in the region. Since its establishment in 2012 A.D., the school has been dedicated to shaping young minds with academic excellence, moral values, and practical skills to help them thrive in a dynamic world. Guided by our motto, "Making Your Child’s World Better," we continuously strive to create a nurturing and inspiring environment where students can grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally.
